* Overview
(site last updated {{{modification-time(%Y %B %d)}}})

I am a postdoctoral research fellow working in the UCLA Department of
Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences (AOS) with [[http://www2.ess.ucla.edu/~mitch/Home.html][Jonathan Mitchell]] of UCLA and
Westmont College and in the California Institute of Technology Division of
Geological and Planetary Sciences (GPS) with [[http://web.gps.caltech.edu/~bordoni/][Simona Bordoni]], where I research
tropical overturning circulations.  Please see my [[file:research.org][Research]] page for more
information.

I am also interested in the [[http://www.openscience.org/blog/][Open Science]] and [[http://www.fsf.org/][Free]] / [[http://opensource.org/][Open Source]] Software
movements, scientific programming using the Python language and Emacs Org-Mode,
and the links between science and aesthetics/culture.  Please see my [[file:resources.org][Resources]]
and [[file:programming.org][Programming]] pages for more information.

Please contact me with any questions about my research or anything else you find
on this site.  Thanks!
